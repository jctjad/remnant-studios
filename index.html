Project #1: Anti-Autoclicker

  • What is autoclicking?
    • Autoclicking is software that automatically clicks for the user at inhuman, high speeds.
    • Autoclicking is an exploit for many online games. Faster clicking speed provides an unfair advantage over users who click manually.
  • The Main Challenge
    • The primary obstacle is identifying manual clicking compared to autoclicking.
    • Because users click at different speeds naturally, the algorithm cannot be based on click speed, but rather other patterns in their clicks.
  • Our Anti-Autoclicker Software
    • Using data collected from users, we developed an algorithm to detect auto-clicking that is not based on clicks per second (CPS).
    • Our software detects autoclicking at speeds even lower than 12cps, which enters the normal range of users' clicking speeds.
    • The algorithm tracks and identifies patterns between groups of clicks of a user.
  • Impact
    • Upon ROBLOX release, our anti-autoclicker immediately replaced the standard “click per second blocker” that was the commonly-used software.
    • 82 clients have used the anti-autoclocker in their personal games.
    • 1,000+ unique users experience the security of the software.
    • There have been 50,000+ visits in games containing the algorithm.
  • Future Improvement
    • In the future, we hope to convert to a machine learning model to better improve the accuracy of the software.
