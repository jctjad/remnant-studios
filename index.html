Product #1: Anti-Autoclicker

  • What is Autoclicking?
    • Autoclicking is software that automatically clicks for the user at inhuman, high speeds.
    • Autoclicking is an exploit for many online games. Faster clicking speeds can provide an unfair advantages over other users.
  • Main Challenge
    • Manual clicking vs. autoclicking: how to distinguish between them.
    • Because users naturally click at different speeds, the algorithm cannot be based on click speed, but rather patterns in their clicks.
  • Our Anti-Autoclicker Software
    • Using data collected from users, we developed an algorithm to detect auto-clicking that is not based on clicks per second (CPS).
    • Our software detects autoclicking at speeds even lower than 12cps, which enters the normal range of users' clicking speeds.
    • The algorithm tracks and identifies patterns between groups of clicks of a user.
  • Impact
    • Upon release on ROBLOX, our anti-autoclicker replaced the CPS limiter that was the old, commonly-used software.
    • 82 clients have used the anti-autoclocker in their personal games, 1,000+ unique users experience the security of the software, and there have been 50,000+ visits in games containing the algorithm.
  • Future Improvement
    • In the future, we hope to convert to a machine learning model to better improve the accuracy of the software.

Product #2: Alt-Detector (WIP)

  • What are Alternate Accounts?
    • Alternate accounts (alts) are secondary accounts made by the same user with the goal of exploiting and cheating inside the game without getting severely punished on their main account.
    • Studies have shown that user toxicity, harassment, and exploitation rates are greatly increased on alternate accounts.
  • Main Challenge
    • Identifying an alternate account in comparison to a main account is difficult. Each game has their own flags and indicators.
  • Our Alt-Detector Bot
    • Our product is a Discord Bot, through which a user can manually check an account to see the percent chance that it is an alt.
    • Our detection algorithm uses machine learning to analyze accounts, and scores them with similar methods as the Google Search algorithm.
  • Future Impact
    • Our product is still a work-in-progress, but on release we hope to reduce the prevelancy of alternate accounts in online gaming.
    • Most importantly, we hope to spread awareness of the problem and encourage reports of suspicious accounts. Every barrier added prevents a subset of illegal activity, a step in the right direction.
  • Project Status
    • This project has been on hold since Summer 2023.
